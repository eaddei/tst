About Us

Our Goal

A community arts school, Emmanuel Music School is dedicated to offering high-quality music experiences to interested people of all ages, skill levels, and financial backgrounds. Since its founding in 2006, Emmanuel Music School has provided weekly access to over 250 students in churches. For students of all ages and skill levels, Emmanuel Music School provides on-site courses, seminars, and private lessons. Stringed instruments, percussion, piano, and voice are just a few of the instruments that our instructors, who are all experienced musicians, specialize in teaching. From complete novices to seasoned musicians honing their skill, our students range widely.

Professor Emmanuel Antwi- Music Background

Emmanuel Oppong Antwi brings approximately 28 years of professional musical experience here as a music instructor, performer, recording vocalist, originator, and songwirter. Emmanuel began his career as a teacher by establishing a music department in Abuakwa, Maakro, in Kumasi, Ghana, where he taught music and directed all the church bands and choir. He subsequently went on to work as a music teacher in many Abuakwa school districts, where he taught instrumental music, band, orchestra, and music education and employment. Emmanuel currently focuses his teaching activities as a private lesson instrumental instructor, providing music classes such as private piano lessons, bass guitar lessons, lead guitar lessons, drum lessons, voice training, and several others at his private school of music in Abuakwa, Maakro, in Kumasi, Ghana. Emmanuel is also involved in the realm of music technology. He creates his own records in his multi-track home recording studio, encompassing a wide spectrum of music, from contemporary music to jazz. These quantum dots include his songs and arrangements, as well as Emmanuel's abilities as a multi-instrumentalist, who plays the piano, string instruments, brass, and drums.
