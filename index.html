Core Classes

We include classes in directing, ear training, harmonization, melodic harmony and rhythm, and sound engineering. These courses form the cornerstone of Emmanuel's musical education programs, and the inducted student competency evaluation and exposure to music innovation test are meant to examine each incoming student's depth of knowledge in either of these core curriculum disciplines. The competency examination and the Prelude to Music Performance test scores allow Emmanuel's Music to enroll each student in the proper conveyance, ear training, harmonization, and music science courses. Students go on to advanced studies in conducting and tonal harmony and counterpoint after successfully completing courses in these primary areas.

Beginner Friendly For Kids and Adults

With private training and frequent group band rehearsals, our skilled music instructors will lead your kid from concept to execution. When giving music lessons to children, every faculty member at Emmanuel Music School is a highly qualified musician who focuses on fundamental music theory and technique. It's never too late to start learning to play an instrument. Our accomplished teachers educate adult students through famous gospel tunes, and they learn to play the instruments in a demanding and enjoyable manner. This program allows pupils to perform onstage at their church or other locations.

Intermediate To Advace

We advance students to more difficult programs at higher ability levels as they develop skills and expertise. Intermediate and advanced students build on the abilities they gained in basic music classes by incorporating more complex techniques and concepts. Emmanuel Music School provides advanced music education for children and adults, teaching via the performance of demanding Ghanaian music styles (highlife, local worship, praises, etc.) and contemporary songs, emphasizing the use of instruments in a live music setting. Our Ghanaian instructors will collaborate with each student to create a personalized learning plan.
