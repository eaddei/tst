Song Writing

For musicians, writing unique music may be among the most pleasurable and fulfilling activities. The songwriting program at Emmanuel Music School assists students in creating creative compositions while teaching the principles of songwriting, arrangement, and music theory. Students receive one regular private session on their chosen instrument, where they study theory, technique, and ideas. These ideas are then put into practice in weekly group courses where students collaborate on lyrical workshops and songwriting activities. Every few months, participants in the Songwriting Program play live or form bands to record their own music.

Performance

Numerous students participate each year in the Performance Program, which is EMS's signature music course. The Performance Program, which employs the proprietary EMS Method, consists of a weekly group practice where young people learn by performing the most well-known songs from jazz, highlife, gospel, and other genres with a band made up of their colleagues. Taking into account their knowledge and capacity to acquire new techniques and ideas, teachers give students songs and parts that are suitable for them. The abilities and ideas introduced by those songs are then further developed by students during weekly private music sessions. This program aims to get youngsters and teenagers ready for live performances. Students from the Performance Program play in the presence of live audiences at local venues periodically.

More Courses

SINGING LESSONS

Millennial singers and aspiring vocalists go to EMS to acquire the basics of singing, such as breathing, posture, and diction. Across pitch training, timbre development, and maintaining a steady pace, our experienced voice instructors aid beginning singers as they proceed through the early phases of vocal development. Intermediate to advanced vocal training from EMS provides expert voice instruction and singing classes to help practicing students achieve their full potential. To expand a student's vocal repertoire, our talented vocal coaches concentrate on sophisticated singing methods. Each program at EMS combines private singing lessons with full-band group rehearsals and live concerts in order to help students reach a greater level of vocal proficiency.

GUITAR LESSONS

During regular private guitar lessons and group rehearsals, our introductory guitar courses spark creativity and encourage young students to develop into world-class guitarists. The underlying premise of EMS is that performance-based music education is the best approach for students to develop musical skills. Everyone of our guitar classes for students contains a performance component. Our intermediate and advanced guitar classes are designed to expand on the concepts that students learned in their introductory sessions and programs. Private guitar lessons are combined with full-band group sessions and stage performances in these curricula, enabling student guitarists to demonstrate their abilities by performing advanced tunes by famous artists. Our world-class guitar instructors' unique teaching method has been shown to improve students' ability to play and achieve at higher levels.

BRASS & PERCUSSION

Take part at Emmanuel Music School, or EMS for short, and see your kid or yourself develop into a brass or percussion musician. Thousands of students have learned to play music using our proprietary EMS approach, both in Ghana and other countries. Our method incorporates spectacular theatrical performance to develop musical skill. By teaching the songs that move them, EMS helps kids begin a lifetime of musical exploration. Our Song First method, which is a component of the EMS Method, teaches theory and technique through well-known songs. Students who use this approach are more likely to be enthusiastic about learning music and enthusiastic about practicing.
